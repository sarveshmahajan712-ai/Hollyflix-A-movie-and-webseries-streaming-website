Frequently Asked Questions


What is Hollyflix ?

Hollyflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries and more - on thousands of internet-connected devices.

You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single ad - all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!


How much does Hollyflix cost ?

Watch Hollyflix on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from ₹149 to ₹649/month.


Where can I watch ?

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your Hollyflix account to watch instantly on the web at netflix.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the Hollyflix app, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, streaming media players and game consoles

You can also download your favourite shows with the iOS or Android app. Use downloads to watch while you're on the go and without an internet connection. Take Hollyflix with you anywhere.


How do I cancel ?

Hollyflix is flexible. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees - start or stop your account anytime.


What can I watch on Hollyflix ?

Netflix has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, shows, anime, award-winning Netflix originals, and more. Watch as much as you want, anytime you want.


Is Hollyflix good for kids ?

The Netflix Kids experience is included in your membership to give parents control while kids enjoy family-friendly TV shows and films in their own space.
